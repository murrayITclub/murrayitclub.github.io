  • In Pixlr: try adding text to something you made with a partial filter or greenscreen.
    Pro tips:
    • If you duplicate your text layer and make it a different color, you can create a cool shadow effect, like this flower picture
    • If you right-click on your text layer, then click Rasterize Layer, you can use filters on the text. Try the Noise filter to make text look sparkly!


  • You can create interesting effects by adding a texture layer, with lower opacity (making it semi-transparent)
