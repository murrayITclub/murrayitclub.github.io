October: Photo Editing

Our first project involves learning to use Pixlr to create memes about class expectations. You can find your own image, or use one of these:

Meme images

When you're done...

  1. Post your meme on your class's board: 3:00 class or 4:00 class
  2. Keep expanding your Pixlr knowledge by...
    • Making more memes for other classes or just for fun
    • Try making a Keep Calm poster
      (click File->New, then set width to 600 and height to 800)
    • Try a tutorial: creating a hybrid animal or putting a picture in words
